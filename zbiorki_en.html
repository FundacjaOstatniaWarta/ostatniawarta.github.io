Donations and foundation support

Thank you for standing on Watch with us!

The mission of the Ostatnia Warta Foundation is to be present for others in the most difficult moments of life – in loneliness, terminal illness, and the dying process. We can fulfill this noble challenge only thanks to the support of people with great hearts. Every gesture of yours matters. We invite you – stand with us on this most important watch.
